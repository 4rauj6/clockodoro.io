# pomodoro-study-appweb
<p>The Pomodoro's study method it's one of the most famous study methods that exists nowadays. In it's core formula, the Pomodoro is a time-management tecniche that has as it's main goal, optimize your studies sections in three different ways: Pomodoro (standard), Short Break (5-10 minutes) and Long Break (20-35 minutes). That method it's highly effective and popular around young students that want to improve their knowlegde and studies times.</p>
